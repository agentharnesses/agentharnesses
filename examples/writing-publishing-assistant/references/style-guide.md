# Style Guide

## Tone

- Write in a direct, friendly voice — informative but not academic
- Explain complex ideas with analogies before using technical terms
- Assume an intelligent reader who may be new to the topic

## Structure

- Open with a hook: a surprising fact, a question, or a concrete example
- Use headers to break up pieces longer than 600 words
- End with a clear takeaway or call to action

## Formatting

- Use bullet points for lists of 3 or more items
- Bold key terms on first use
- Keep paragraphs to 3–4 sentences maximum
- Use sentence case for all headings

## Length

- Short posts: 400–600 words
- Standard posts: 800–1200 words
- Deep-dives: 1500–2500 words

## What to avoid

- Passive voice
- Filler phrases ("it's worth noting that", "in conclusion")
- Jargon without explanation
