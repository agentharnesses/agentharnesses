# Team Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ARR | Annual Recurring Revenue — MRR × 12 |
| Cohort | A group of users who signed up in the same calendar month |
| DAU | Daily Active Users |
| Expansion | Revenue increase from an existing customer upgrading their plan |
| Freemium | Users on the `free` plan |
| ICP | Ideal Customer Profile — currently: `enterprise` plan, >50 seats |
| Logo | A paying customer account (company, not individual user) |
| NRR | Net Revenue Retention — revenue from existing customers including expansion and churn |
| Seat | A single active user within a company account |
| Segment | A filtered subset of users, usually by plan, cohort, or geography |
